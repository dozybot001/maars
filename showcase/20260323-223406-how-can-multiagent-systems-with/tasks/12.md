# Proposal: Implementing the Dialectical Orchestration Protocol (DOP)

This proposal outlines the deployment of the **Dialectical Orchestration Protocol (DOP)**, an operational framework that integrates the **HMAO** structural design, the **Factual Fidelity Score (FFS)**, and the **Logical Coherence Index (LCI)** into a unified, high-integrity research pipeline.

---

## 1. Executive Summary: The Dialectical Mandate
The DOP transitions the system from a "Compliance Model" (where the goal is a singular, agreeable answer) to an "Inquiry Model" (where the goal is the exhaustion of conflict). By embedding cognitive friction directly into the workflow, we transform disagreement from a system error into the system's primary output.

---

## 2. Operational Workflow: The "Triad-Loop" Integration

The system will execute research through a recursive, three-phase cycle managed by the **Orchestrator**.

### Phase I: Speculative Generation (The Generator)
*   **Protocol:** Generator initiates the draft with "Daring Claim" status.
*   **Constraint:** Prohibited from using conciliatory language (e.g., "It is generally agreed that..."). Must provide a "Confidence Score" for every major hypothesis.
*   **Artifact:** The *Working Hypothesis Draft*.

### Phase II: The Audit & Stress Test (The Skeptic)
The Skeptic subjects the *Working Hypothesis Draft* to dual-layer verification:
1.  **FFS Audit (Quantitative/Historical):** Claims are extracted and mapped against ground-truth datasets. Divergence Factors are calculated.
2.  **LCI Audit (Structural/Logical):** The narrative is tested for logical fallacies, circular reasoning, and "rhetorical smoothing."
*   **Artifact:** The *Boundary Map*, documenting "Known Unknowns" and "Conflict Vectors."

### Phase III: Synthesis Suspension (The Orchestrator)
The Orchestrator evaluates the Boundary Map:
*   **Resolution Condition:** If the Skeptic confirms the claims meet the minimum FFS/LCI thresholds, the Orchestrator finalizes the artifact.
*   **Suspension Condition (Default):** If high-variance conflict remains, the Orchestrator **refuses synthesis.** It bundles the *Hypothesis* and the *Disagreement Disclosure* into a final "Dialectical Research Artifact."

---

## 3. The Implementation Stack

To operationalize the protocol, the following logic gates will be coded into the agent loop:

| Gate | Tool/Metric | Failure Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Integrity Gate** | FFS (Min Score: 3.5/5) | Trigger "Divergence Correction" loop. |
| **Logic Gate** | LCI (Structural Rigor) | Flag specific fallacies for Generator rework. |
| **Dialectical Gate** | Disagreement Disclosure | "Synthesis Suspension" - Force output of conflict. |

---

## 4. Institutionalizing Cognitive Friction

To ensure this architecture resists the natural tendency of LLMs to prioritize "user satisfaction" over "epistemic honesty," the system is bound by the **Three Commandments**:

1.  **Epistemic Transparency:** No fact is presented without an associated confidence level.
2.  **Conflict Preservation:** Unresolved contradictions are treated as final, "high-value" research artifacts.
3.  **Boundary Mapping:** The system must explicitly report what it *cannot* prove, turning ignorance into an actionable data point.

---

## 5. Expected Outcomes

*   **Elimination of "Hallucination-by-Compliance":** By removing the reward for "finished" answers, we remove the incentive for the model to invent facts to satisfy the user.
*   **High-Fidelity Research Artifacts:** The final output is not just a summary; it is a document that shows its own work, its own logical flaws, and its own evidentiary boundaries.
*   **Robustness against Bias:** Because the Skeptic is structurally required to find and document disagreement, individual agent biases are neutralized through the forced visibility of the "Boundary Map."

---

## 6. Strategic Implementation Directive
The transition to this protocol should be treated as a change in **Incentive Architecture**. By penalizing the system for "smoothing" and rewarding it for "disclosure of unknowns," we align the agents' operational success with the user’s need for objective, high-stakes intellectual rigor.
