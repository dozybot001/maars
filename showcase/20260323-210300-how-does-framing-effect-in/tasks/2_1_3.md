To synthesize the interaction between media framing and cognitive architecture, we must apply the **Cognitive Buffer Hypothesis**. This hypothesis posits that the "Translation Cost"—the mental effort required to bridge the gap between a news frame and an internal cognitive heuristic—determines a reader's susceptibility to the information presented.

### The Buffer Susceptibility Matrix

This matrix predicts the likelihood that a specific Framing Axis (from Task 2_1_1) will be accepted and internalized by the reader without triggering a "rejection" or "re-encoding" response by their Cognitive Buffer.

| Framing Axis | Analytic/Explicit (Nisbett/Hall) | Holistic/Implicit (Nisbett/Hall) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Episodic** | **High Susceptibility** | **Low Susceptibility** |
| **Thematic** | **Moderate Susceptibility** | **High Susceptibility** |
| **Conflict/Binary** | **High Susceptibility** | **Low Susceptibility** |

---

### I. Analysis of Susceptibility Levels

#### 1. Episodic Framing (Focal Object Focus)
*   **Analytic (High):** Matches the Analytic preference for "Object-Property" extraction. The frame provides a clear "who" and "what," allowing for immediate categorization. The Buffer is bypassed because the input is highly compatible with the existing mental schema.
*   **Holistic (Low):** Triggers a "Contextual Vacuum." The Holistic Buffer detects the omission of the "field." The reader experiences this as "incomplete" or "shallow," leading to a rejection of the frame and a forced shift toward external, secondary information-seeking.

#### 2. Thematic Framing (Field/System Focus)
*   **Analytic (Moderate):** Presents a "Translation Cost." The Analytic Buffer struggles with the lack of a clear, discrete "object" to attach blame or praise to. They often view thematic reporting as "opinionated" or "vague," requiring mental effort to distill the report into a singular "fact."
*   **Holistic (High):** Aligns with the Holistic preference for systemic interdependence. The frame provides the "field" and the relational dynamics. The Buffer accepts the information as "meaningful" and "congruent" with their perception of reality, leading to high internalization.

#### 3. Conflict/Binary Framing (The Logic of Parts)
*   **Analytic (High):** The Binary Frame acts as an "Analytic Anchor." It provides a clear, discrete opposition, which is the preferred method for resolving complexity in Western-tradition logic. The Buffer readily integrates the information because the "Us vs. Them" logic is compatible with dispositional attribution.
*   **Holistic (Low):** Triggers the "Mismatch Dissonance." Because the Holistic processor views conflict as a symptom of a larger systemic imbalance, the binary "solution" (choosing a winner) is perceived as an intellectual failure. The reader’s Buffer rejects the binary constraint as a violation of the "whole" truth, often leading to a total dismissal of the source’s credibility.

---

### II. Analytical Implications

1.  **The "Buffer Breach":** When a frame has **Low Susceptibility**, the Buffer does not merely ignore the information; it actively re-interprets it to maintain internal cognitive consistency. For instance, a Holistic reader encountering a binary frame will perform a "Systemic Override," reframing the conflict as an "evolutionary adjustment" or "systemic cycle," thereby neutralizing the "high-alert" impact intended by the frame.
2.  **The Feedback Loop:** Media outlets typically design for the **Analytic/High Susceptibility** cohort because the Analytic feedback loop (click rates on "focal objects") provides cleaner data metrics. This effectively starves the Holistic processor of "high-susceptibility" framing, forcing them into peripheral information networks that are specifically tuned to their "Thematic/Field-based" requirements.
3.  **Predictive Polarization:** This matrix demonstrates that polarization is not just a result of *what* is being said, but the *structural mismatch* between the frame and the Buffer. As long as media institutions prioritize **Episodic/Conflict framing**, they essentially forfeit the trust of the Holistic-cognitive demographic, ensuring that this group will remain sequestered in alternative information ecosystems that align with their cognitive "field" requirement.
